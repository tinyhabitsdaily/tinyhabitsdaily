1. Introduction

‚ñº

Welcome to TinyHabitsDaily ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices for the TinyHabitsDaily app.

Important: TinyHabitsDaily is designed with privacy by design. All your personal habit data remains on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal habit information.

2. Information We Collect

‚ñº

2.1 Personal Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Habit Data: Your habits, goals, and progress data stays entirely on your device. We never collect or store this information.
  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • User Content: Any notes, journals, or personal content you create in the app remains private on your device.

2.2 Anonymous Analytics Only

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous, aggregated data about app usage patterns (no personal identification possible)
  • Technical Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version (for compatibility purposes)
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical information when the app experiences errors (to improve stability)

Important: All analytics data is completely anonymous and cannot be linked back to you personally.

3. How We Use Your Information

‚ñº

Since we don't collect personal data, here's how we use the minimal anonymous analytics:

  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage patterns help us understand which features are most valuable
  • Bug Fixes: Crash reports help us identify and fix technical issues
  • Performance Optimization: Anonymous metrics help us improve app speed and reliability
  • Feature Development: Understanding how the app is used (anonymously) guides new feature development

What we DON'T do:

  • We don't track your habit progress or personal data
  • We don't send marketing emails or notifications based on your data
  • We don't create profiles or target you with advertising
  • We don't sell any information to third parties

4. Data Storage and Security

‚ñº

Your Data Stays on Your Device: All your personal habit data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure data storage mechanisms. We never transmit or store your personal habit information on our servers.

Security Measures:

  • Local Encryption: Your habit data is encrypted on your device using iOS security features
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't store your personal data in the cloud or on our servers
  • Anonymous Analytics Only: The minimal analytics we collect are anonymized before transmission
  • No Account Required: No sign-up or login required, so no personal identifiers are collected

Data Control: Since your habit data never leaves your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete the app at any time to remove all your data.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

‚ñº

We Don't Share Personal Information Because We Don't Collect It: Since we don't collect any personal information, there's nothing personal to share, sell, or trade.

Anonymous Analytics Only: The only data we collect is completely anonymous analytics, which may be shared with:

  • Apple Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics through Apple's built-in analytics (if you have analytics enabled in iOS settings)
  • Crash Reporting Services: Anonymous technical crash reports to help improve app stability

Important: This anonymous data cannot be traced back to you personally and contains no habit information or personal details.

Legal Requirements: In the extremely unlikely event of legal requirements, we would have no personal information to disclose since we don't collect any.

6. Third-Party Services

‚ñº

Our app may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Analytics (for iOS users)
  • Crash reporting services

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

7. Your Rights and Choices

‚ñº

Complete Control Over Your Data: Since your habit data stays on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: All your habit data is accessible within the app at any time
  • Modification: You can modify, update, or delete any habit data within the app
  • Complete Deletion: Delete the app to completely remove all your data from your device
  • Data Export: Export your habit data from the app if desired (stays local to you)
  • Analytics Opt-out: Disable analytics in your iOS settings under Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements

No Account Deletion Needed: Since we don't create accounts or store your data, there's nothing to delete on our end. Your privacy is protected by design.
