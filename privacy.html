1. Introduction

Welcome to TinyHabitsDaily ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the TinyHabitsDaily app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Habit Data: Information about your habits, goals, and progress that you enter into the app
  • User Preferences: Settings and customizations you make within the app
  • Feedback: Any feedback, comments, or suggestions you provide to us

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: Information about how you use the app, including features accessed and time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app experiences errors or crashes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the TinyHabitsDaily app functionality
  • Track your habit progress and provide personalized insights
  • Improve our app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send you important updates about the app
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services

4. Data Storage and Security

Your habit data is primarily stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by our team

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (such as analytics services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Analytics (for iOS users)
  • Crash reporting services

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access your habit data within the app
  • Correction: You can modify or update your information at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your habits and data within the app
  • Data Export: You can export your habit data from the app
  • Opt-out: You can disable analytics in the app settings

8. Children's Privacy

TinyHabitsDaily is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

9. International Users

If you are using TinyHabitsDaily from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers are located.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users of significant changes through the app or email
  • Post the updated policy on our website

Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide you with our services and for legitimate business purposes. You can delete your data at any time through the app settings.

Contact Us
